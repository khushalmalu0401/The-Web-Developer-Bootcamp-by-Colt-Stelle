Scam 1992: The Harshad Mehta Story is an Indian Hindi-language financial thriller streaming television series on SonyLIV directed by Hansal Mehta, with Jai Mehta serving as the co-director.[1] Based on 1992 Indian stock market scam committed by many stockbrokers including Harshad Mehta, the series is adapted from journalist Sucheta Dalal and Debashish Basu's 1992 book The Scam: Who Won, Who Lost, Who Got Away.[2][3] The screenplay and dialogues were written by Sumit Purohit, Saurabh Dey, Vaibhav Vishal and Karan Vyas.
